Trying Capoeira can be a great way to experience the rich cultural heritage and physical benefits that this Afro-Brazilian martial art offers.

Capoeira originated in Brazil during the 16th century among African slaves. Forced to work on sugarcane plantations, they used their traditional combat and dance forms for self-defense. To disguise their martial practice, they combined fighting movements with music and dance, creating Capoeira. Over time, Capoeira evolved, incorporating elements from various cultures.

It is common for Capoeira groups and schools to promote events, workshops, and classes in various communities, including Vidigal. Capoeira events can include demonstrations, performances, workshops, and cultural celebrations. If you're exploring Rio de Janeiro and looking to experience the vibrant world of Capoeira, consider trying a session at Projeto Vidigal Capoeira.
